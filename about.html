About StackOverflow-lite

StackOverflow-lite allows you to ask questions and let other users provide you with answers.

You can also provide answers to questions posted by other users.

As a user, you are able to:

  • Create an account and log in.
  • post questions.
  • post answers
  • Accept the preferred answer
  • upvote or downvote any answer
  • search for questions on the platform
